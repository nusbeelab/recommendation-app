This study consists of 3 parts. Each part has 60 questions.

In each question, you will be asked to choose between two options of payment, which can be either certain or uncertain. Payment is presented in the unit of points, 100 points = USD 2.

  • If the payment is certain, it will be described as “X with certainty”;
  • If the payment is uncertain, all possible outcomes and corresponding possibilities will be explicitly described in the form of “X1 with probability P1”.

Here is an example.

50 with certainty
0 with probability 0.5
100 with probability 0.5
A
B
  • If you choose Option A, you receive 50 points with certainty.
  • If you choose Option B, you receive 0 point with probability 0.5 and receive 100 points with probability 0.5.
