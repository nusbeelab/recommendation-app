{{ elif participant.treatment == "R_CF" }}

In Part 2, there are 60 questions. Similar to Part 1, you will be asked to choose between two options in each question.

The difference in Part 2 is that you will receive a recommendation in each question.

The recommendation is based on decisions from participants who are similar to you.

  • In a previous study, we recruited a US representative sample from Prolific and asked participants to answer the same questions as in the current study.
  • Based on your choices in Part 1, the system will identify a group of participants who had made similar decisions as you.
  • For each question in Part 2, the system will recommend an option to you according to similar participants' decisions in this question.
  • It is entirely up to you whether you want to follow or not to follow the recommendation for each question.
